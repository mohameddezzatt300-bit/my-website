Case Study 02 Industry Home Decor Category Door Mats Goal Website Sales Markets EG · KSA · UAE · KW · QA

Pioneering a category
no one was running.

Took over the account in July 2024 for a completely new niche: premium door mats. Unique product, no direct competitors, premium price — and a target audience whose CPMs could surpass 150 EGP. We built the playbook from scratch.

Background
& challenge.

01 · What we walked into
01

Brand-new niche

Took over in July 2024 in a category the brand was pioneering. The product was unique and unfamiliar to the market.

02

No direct competitors

Market pioneer position. Blessing and a curse — no lookalike benchmarks, no audiences to clone from competitors.

03

Premium pricing

EGP 2,000–6,000 per unit. Upscale customer, which meant CPMs that could surpass 150 EGP and long consideration windows.

04

Inconsistent order costs

CAC was volatile. We had to stabilize it before scaling, not during.

05

In-stream fraud

In-stream ads were generating fake orders. We shut that placement down and reallocated — a 2-day decision, not a 2-week debate.

Key advantages.

02 · What we had going for us
01

High-quality content

Executed flawlessly. The #1 driver of success — great creative covered a multitude of sins elsewhere.

02

Client support

The client provided everything needed — access, assets, decisions. A fast, aligned operator is worth more than any framework.

03

Variety

Wide selection of products suitable for any space — indoor & outdoor — which widened the creative surface area dramatically.

04

Customization

Customers could design their own styles at higher price points — a natural AOV lever and a retention hook.

05

Diverse occasions

Diverse styles encouraged customers to collect different mats for different occasions — repeat purchase built into the product.

The strategy.

03 · The playbook
01

Daily testing discipline

Regularly experimented with different audiences, interests, and creative assets. Tests don't run themselves — they need a rhythm.

02

Platform focus

Emphasized platforms that consistently produced reliable results rather than spreading spend thinly for the optics of omni-channel.

03

ADV+ audiences

Leaned into Advantage+ shopping campaigns where the algorithm could outrun our hand-built audiences — strong results once we trusted it.

04

ABO Multi Ads Flexible

Campaign structures shouldn't rely on a single approach — flexible frameworks are crucial for strong accounts. ABO Multi Ads Flexible was the workhorse here.

04 — Outcomes

Expansion & growth.

After strong Egypt results, we expanded into Saudi Arabia, UAE, Kuwait and Qatar — each market tuned on its own CPM curve, creative language and fulfillment cadence.
