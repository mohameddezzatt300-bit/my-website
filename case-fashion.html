Case Study 01 Industry Fashion Category Men's · eCommerce Goal Website Sales Market Egypt

From zero site to a
niche DTC engine.

The brand had no website, hesitant leadership, and customer conversations happening over DMs. We built the infrastructure, seeded a clean data layer from day one, and cracked a hard-to-reach 35–45 demo across Cairo, Alexandria and the North Coast.

The challenge.

01 · Starting position
01

No website initially

The brand didn't have a website at all. We took the initiative to build one so we could own the funnel and collect first-party data.

02

Client hesitation

Leadership was hesitant about committing to a web presence. We made the case, set expectations, and proceeded.

03

DM-only communication

Before the site, customer communication was limited to DMs. No funnel, no retargeting pool, no LTV visibility.

04

Hard demo to crack

Targeting a clothing-focused 35–45 audience was difficult. Interest stacks were noisy and creative fatigue was fast.

05

UX was the bottleneck

Made significant changes to the website to make it more accessible and user-friendly for the target demographic — not just a traffic problem.

The strategy.

02 · How we played it
01

Trending + unique product focus

Leaned into trending, differentiated products that weren't already available in the market — not another lookalike catalogue.

02

Work with what you've got

The client couldn't provide images or videos at scale, so I worked with a small group of designers and used every available asset efficiently — recutting, restructuring, re-framing.

03

Instagram-first

Focused exclusively on Instagram initially given the follower base and Facebook's poor performance for this audience.

04

Upscale geo-targeting

Targeted upscale zones in Cairo, Alexandria, and the North Coast — a price-point match for the 2,000–6,000 EGP AOV.

05

Competitor & market analysis

Thorough competitor and market analysis to address the audience uniquely — positioning first, ads second.

03 — Outcomes

The results.

A clean foundation produced a compounding curve. New demo, new channel, new infrastructure — all working in the same direction.
